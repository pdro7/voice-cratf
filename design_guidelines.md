# Design Guidelines: AI Voice Agent Landing Page

## Design System

**Color Palette:**
- Deep Navy: `#0f172a` (primary text)
- Soft White: `#fafafa` (background)
- Electric Blue: `#3b82f6` (CTAs)
- Subtle Purple: `#8b5cf6` (accents)
- Light Gray: `#f1f5f9` (section backgrounds)

**Typography:**
- Font Family: Inter (or similar clean sans-serif)
- Generous spacing throughout
- Modern, elegant hierarchy

**Visual Treatment:**
- Smooth gradients: Navy to purple, subtle applications
- Soft shadows on cards and elevated elements
- Border radius: 8-12px consistently
- Glass-morphism effects on card components

**Layout Philosophy:**
- Generous white space - premium SaaS aesthetic (Stripe/Linear inspiration)
- Clean, sophisticated presentation
- Full-width sections with centered, constrained content

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero Section
- Full-width, centered content
- **Headline:** "Your 24/7 AI Receptionist That Never Takes a Break"
- **Subheadline:** "AI voice agents and avatars that handle customer calls, book appointments, and answer questions around the clock - while you focus on growing your business."
- **CTAs:** 
  - Primary: "Try Our Demo Agent" (electric blue, bold treatment)
  - Secondary: "Book a Free Consultation" (outlined, navy text)
- **Trust Line:** "Serving businesses across Europe • GDPR Compliant • Built in EU" (small, gray text positioned below CTAs)
- **Background:** Modern AI/technology visual or abstract gradient background

### 2. Demo Section
- Light gray background (`#f1f5f9`)
- Card-style layout with soft shadow
- **Heading:** "Experience It Yourself"
- **Phone Number:** "+34 XXX XXX XXX" - prominent display, large, clickable
- **Subtext:** "Call now and talk to our AI agent - see how natural it sounds"
- Video embed placeholder or interactive demo area

### 3. Services Section
- 3 equal-width columns with card layout
- Glass-morphism effect on cards
- Purple accent on hover with smooth animations
- Each card contains icon, heading, description:
  1. 🎙️ **Voice Agents** - "Answer calls, book appointments, qualify leads - automatically"
  2. 👤 **AI Avatars** - "Training videos, customer support, course instructors"
  3. 🔄 **Smart Automation** - "Connect to your calendar, CRM, and existing tools"

### 4. Use Cases Section
- Light gray background (`#f1f5f9`)
- **Heading:** "Who We Help"
- 2x2 grid layout (4 cards total):
  - Healthcare practices: "Never miss patient calls"
  - Real estate agents: "Handle property inquiries 24/7"
  - Professional services: "Always available for clients"
  - Online educators: "Scale your teaching"
- Each card with icon and brief description

### 5. How It Works Section
- 3-step horizontal timeline
- Visual timeline connector with purple gradient
- Steps:
  1. **Discovery Call** - "We understand your needs"
  2. **Build & Test** - "Custom AI agent for your business"
  3. **Launch & Optimize** - "Go live and improve continuously"

### 6. CTA Section
- Navy background with gradient overlay
- White text for contrast
- **Headline:** "Ready to Never Miss a Customer Again?"
- **Primary CTA:** "Get Started Today" (large, prominent)
- **Secondary:** "Schedule Free Demo"

### 7. Footer
- Simple layout, navy text
- Contact info placeholder
- Social links placeholder
- Copyright: "© 2024 - Built in EU"

## Interactions & Animations
- Smooth scroll animations between sections
- Card hover states with subtle elevation changes
- Modern, elegant transitions (avoid jarring movements)
- Fully responsive mobile design with stacked layouts

## Images
- **Hero Section:** Large background image or abstract gradient showcasing AI/technology theme - modern, sophisticated visual treatment
